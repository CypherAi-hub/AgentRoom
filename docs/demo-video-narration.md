# Demo Video Narration — Agent Room

Target: ~150 words, 75 seconds, ~120 wpm. Tone like Linear or Cursor — confident, calm, no hedging, no marketing fluff. Voiced as a single voice, recorded dry. **Bolded** words get the emphasis beat.

---

## Word-for-word script

(0:00–0:05, Shot 01 — title card)

Most AI agents stop at chat. We **didn't**.

(0:05–0:12, Shot 02 — typing the prompt)

Agent Room gives every agent a **real cloud desktop** — keyboard, mouse, browser, files. You give it a sentence.

(0:12–0:25, Shot 03 — VM comes alive)

A fresh sandbox **boots in seconds**. The agent opens a browser, navigates the web, and starts working — exactly like a person would.

(0:25–0:35, Shot 04 — activity drawer)

Every click, every keystroke, every screenshot **streams live**. Nothing is hidden behind a chat bubble.

(0:35–0:45, Shot 05 — done)

When it finishes, you get the artifact and the **receipts** — the full trail of what your agent actually did.

(0:45–0:55, Shot 06 — /runs)

Replay any run. Audit it, share it, debug it. **One link**, every step.

(0:55–1:05, Shot 07 — pricing)

Pay for the work, **not for seats**. Top up, run, done.

(1:05–1:15, Shot 08 — close)

**Stop watching screens. Start shipping work.** Agent Room — agentroom.app.

## Word count

Approximately 153 words. Read at a confident 122 wpm with the indicated pauses on each shot boundary, the script lands at 75 seconds with ~1.5 seconds of breathing room before the closing CTA hold.

## Delivery notes

- Pause for **half a beat** at each em-dash. Pause a **full beat** between shots — the visual cut is the rhythm.
- Do **not** smile through the line "We didn't." — flat, certain, then a half-beat of silence.
- "Real cloud desktop", "boots in seconds", "streams live", "receipts", "one link", "not for seats", and the final closing line are the load-bearing emphasis beats. Everything else is connective tissue — keep it light.
- Record three takes per shot block. Comp the best line reads.
- Mix voice -3 dB above the music bed. Sidechain the music -4 dB under the voice.
